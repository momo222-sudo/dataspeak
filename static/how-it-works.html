Turn messy data into a clear, decision-ready report in under a minute

DataSpeak takes raw SQL output, spreadsheets, R results, or pandas dataframes and turns them into plain-English summaries, prioritized recommendations, red-flag alerts, and ready-to-send emails — backed by real numbers, not guesses.

How it works

Three steps from raw output to a polished report your team can act on immediately.

1

Paste your data

Copy results straight from SQL, Excel, R, Python/pandas, or any table. Tell DataSpeak who the report is for — CEO, client, board, or your team — and pick the industry for relevant benchmarks.

2

AI analyzes & verifies

Our model reads every row and column, checks the numbers against industry benchmarks, and shows its arithmetic for every percentage or trend it reports — no invented figures.

3

Get a complete report

Receive a plain-English summary, key insights tagged as findings or assumptions, recommendations, red flags, and an email draft — exportable to Word or slides, or shared via a live link.

What you actually get

Every report shows its work — real numbers, transparent confidence levels, and a risk read you can trust.
